Russia: A story of great victories and hard trials

Choose those great Russian victories that inspire you:

  • Napoleon fled Russia... in a women's fur coat – his army was so frozen that the French even looted church vestments.
  • Russia could have bought Alaska back – there was a secret clause in the 1867 treaty about a possible ransom, but the United States ignored it.
  • Peter the Great introduced a tax on beards – he wanted to make Russia "European", and the boyars paid for the right to wear the Russian tradition. 
  • Ivan the Terrible did NOT kill his son – Repin's famous painting is based on rumors, but there is no real evidence.
